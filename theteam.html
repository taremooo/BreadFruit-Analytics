OUR TEAM

REX OMONODE

Dr. Omonode received his Ph.D. from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where his doctoral research focused on developing multivariate and spatial statistical models to better understand relationships among variables in time and space. In the last 20+ years, Dr. Omonode has worked as Research Scientist in both public and private sectors and applied data analytics to gain insights and provide solutions to seeming intractable problems for more efficient resource management and profitability. It is this wealth of experience and passion for data analytics that Dr. Omonode brings to Breadfruit Analytics Ltd to help governments and their agencies, businesses, NGOs and individuals to gain actionable insights and make fact-based decisions to improve efficiency of resource allocation, mobilization and profitability.
