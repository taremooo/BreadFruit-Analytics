Lets update you about health!

Breadfruit works with health insurers, hospitals, life-science organizations and regulatory agencies to harness the power of data for improving outcomes, reducing risk, and forecasting changes.


We work with doctors, public health professionals and researchers to develop algorithms/models to help diagnostics and reduce diagnostics errors.


For example, algorithms that assist doctors and nurses to more accurately diagnose typhoid vs. malaria and assist public health professionals to forecast disease infection rates in a given population.
